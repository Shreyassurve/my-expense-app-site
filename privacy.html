Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 Nov 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Expense Combiner mobile application ("we", "our", "the app") handles user information.

Information We Collect

  • Google Authentication: The app uses Google Sign-In only to authenticate you securely. We do NOT collect, store, or share your Google account information.
  • Local Expenses Data: Your expense information is stored locally on your own device only. We do NOT upload your data to any server.

How We Use Information

Your information is used only to provide access to the app and allow you to save expenses locally.

Third-Party Services

The app uses:

  • Google OAuth (for login)
  • No analytics
  • No advertising

Data Storage

All saved expense data remains on your device. You can delete the app at any time to remove all data.

Your Consent

By using the Expense Combiner application, you consent to this privacy policy.
